# RODs + Shondra + Clinic Leads Tasks

## After Lease Signed
- Determine if this will be a virtual front desk location; inform Strategic Growth
- Hire new staff and start new staff training (if not started beforehand)
- Verify that front desk, office staff, and MAs can be hired and trained in time; confirm with Strategic Growth that opening date is feasible

## 1 Month Before Opening
- Set up ultrasound training for staff

## Opening Day
- Check out proper keys and key cards to each staff member; keep a list of who has what keys/key cards
- Do Unified door training (how to arm/disarm alarm, set door open for certain hours, re-lock, use Unified door system)
- Confirm iPads are logged into correct accounts and working
- Confirm ultrasound uploads are working and images uploading to correct location in NextTech
- Walk through waiting area — check cleanliness and seating before opening to patients
- Ensure downtime packets are printed and accessible (in case internet goes down)
- Observe patient check-in process and give feedback
- Shadow provider's first consult — observe and give feedback
- Confirm patient movement flow is smooth (waiting room → scan → provider → checkout)
- Identify any bottlenecks or delays and address them
- Provide breakfast for the team on the first day
- Hold team huddle at start of day (front desk, sonographers, provider)
- Review processes with front desk — ensure they can apply training and are ready
- Print out Mercy Manual and put in red binder
- Ensure everyone in clinic knows where Mercy Kit is located and what's in it
- Get safe set up and go over safe protocol

## First Week After Opening
- Finish receiving and checking in all deliveries on supply check-in sheet
- Identify any additional supplies needed that haven't been ordered; add to supply check-in sheet
- Verify daily ultrasound images are uploading consistently to PACS
- Audit image quality
- Confirm cleaning staff schedule and that clinic team knows when they come in
- Review supply inventory process, how to use inventory sheet, and how to reorder supplies with clinic team; confirm who is in charge of inventory at this location (coordinate with Kurt)
- Confirm consents are being completed properly in NextTech
- Provide summary of first week observations to Strategic Growth (feedback on improvements)

## First Month After Opening
- Review patient feedback and wait times
- Confirm techs are using correct measurements and protocols
- Review abnormal studies with providers
- Identify top 5 operational gaps
- Review scheduling patterns (no-shows, bottlenecks)
