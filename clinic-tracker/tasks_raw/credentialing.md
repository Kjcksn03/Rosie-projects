# Credentialing Tasks

## When New Provider is Hired
- Send welcome email to new provider and start collecting credentialing documents

## After Lease Signed
- Initiate out-of-network linking to PPO plans
- If applicable (e.g. California): start credentialing enrollment for HMOs
- Start Medicare credentialing enrollment for the provider

## 1 Week Before Opening
- Confirm what plans the provider is fully linked with and participating in
- Share confirmed plan participation with Operations and Strategic Growth
